TOP 1

Kelingking Beach

Located on Nusa Penida Island, Kelingking Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches and a favorite photo spot for tourists when visiting the island. The entrance to this beach is on a cliff with a hill that extends and juts out into the sea. If you want to enjoy the beauty of Kelingking Beach, tourists have to struggle a little. They will climb down a steep cliff with a slope of around 70-80 degrees and hold on to a wooden fence. However, they can also use a boat to get to this beach. One of the activities you can do at Kelingking Beach apart from enjoying the view is diving and observing dozens of manta rays at Manta Point.

TOP 2

Nusa Dua Beach

One of the reviewers on the Travelers' Choice Best of the Best 2021 page praised Nusa Dua Beach as a beautiful beach with clean sand. Not only that, Nusa Dua Beach is also considered to have calm waves, making it suitable for family recreation and relaxing. This beach is in the Nusa Dua area where a number of hotels have been built with various facilities. One of the tourist destinations in the area is Water Blow. If you visit there, tourists can see the view of sea water spraying into the air emerging from the coral crevices.

TOP 3

Sanur Beach

Sanur Beach is one of the famous beaches in Bali. Because, this beach is one of the favorite locations to enjoy the view of the sunrise. One of the reviews on the Travelers' Choice Best of the Best 2021 awards page also revealed that Sanur Beach is a place for sunrise seekers. Tourists can also do other activities while at this location, including fishing, cycling along the coastline, and visiting the Le Meyeur Museum which contains works of art by the Belgian artist, Adrien-Jean Le Mayeur.
